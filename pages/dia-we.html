Target User

  • Type 2 diabetes who are newly diagnosed (35-45 yrs old)
  • Family caregivers

Project Statement

Provide a platform to strengthen the relationship between newly diagnosed
type 2 diabetes and their family,so as to let the diabetics feel love and
support instead of fighting the disease alone.

01.

日常任務

Daily Task

Use the form of tasks to remind patients to record their illnesses and encourage them to eat healthy and exercise. After linking the account, the family can know the patient's condition

02.

聊天室

Industry News

Let patients or family members express their negative emotions by chatting with pets

03.

膳食

Meal

Check what menu is suitable for diabetics.
Family members can prepare meals for patient through tasks.

04.

運動

Exercise

Pets encourage patients to perform exercise-related tasks with family members. Various games are combined with exercise, which is very interesting and makes patients and their families more willing to exercise.

05.

寵物之家

Pet’s Home

Patient’s pet and family’s pet live together. Users can use the money obtained from completing tasks to buy clothes for pets or buy furniture to decorate their home. Cute pets and various game modes attract users to continue using the app.

Pet Status
