Project Statement

To create a barrier-free information platform for the visually impaired. Through interaction with like-minded individuals and volunteers, we encourage the visually impaired to actively participate in and organize various activities to develop their interests, thereby promoting social inclusion.

Project Objectives

Social Connection
Integrated Society
Barrier-Free Environment
  • Organize activities to encourage VIP to develop an interest -> build up confidence
  • Encouragement and care from people around visually impaired
  • Promote communication between users with rewards
  • Provide related functions to promote social interaction
  • User autonomy
  • The activity is accessible to both VIP and normal people
  • Text / voice input mainly
  • Image Description
  • Simple layout

01 About Visual Impairment

Types of Visual Impairment

  • 1. Total Blindness
  • 2. Low Vision
  • 3. Color Blindness
  • 4. Visual Field Loss
  • 5. Night Blindness

Accessible UI Design for the Visually Impaired

  • 1. High Contrast Colors
  • 2. Adjustable Font Sizes
  • 3. Clear Navigation
  • 4. Avoid Flickering and Overly Animated Elements
  • 5. Avoid using pure white

04Exhibition

Graduation Exhibition

We use tactile paving to attract visitors' attention and guide them over. The booth is divided into two sections: the information area and the workshop.

The information area introduces the features of our app, while the workshop allows visitors to blindfold themselves and paint reusable bag, enabling them to experience the inconveniences faced by visually impaired individuals while also receiving an reusable bag.

Exhibition in collaboration with The Hong Kong Society for the Blind
