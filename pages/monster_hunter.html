Target User

University students who are stressful

Project Statement

TMONSTER HUNTER allows user to release their stress by playing a mini game in a private place. This game required player to yell in certain decibel to defeat monster. As a reward, a throat lozenge and collectible card, which contains tips on stress management, will rebate to player.

01 Design Concept

A Review of Academic Stress among Hong Kong Undergraduate Students A survey involving 1200 university students in Hong Kong, 68.5% of participants reported mild to severe depression symptoms, and 54.4% reported mild to severe symptoms of anxiety.
