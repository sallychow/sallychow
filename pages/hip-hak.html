Project Statement

Hip Hak targets young people who enjoy hip-hop music. Since hip-hop shares similarities with Hakka folk songs, we aim to use hip-hop as a bridge to promote Hakka food culture through music. Our goal is to raise awareness of Hakka culture and attract more interest from the youth.

故事

Story

We've created a story that engages participants and enhances our activities.

Story:
The Hakka's health preservation methods are infused into four elemental stones, each guarded by a divine beast. A musician interested in these methods gathers friends to accomplish the mission, determined to master the Hakka approach to health.

Hakka cuisine focuses on seasonal ingredients, mainly using poultry and game. Thus, we assign the divine beasts as 春雞 夏狗 秋鵝 冬羊

04.

擂殿

Lei Dian

Lei Tea Workshop. You can redeem a free cup of lei tea and participate in the workshop here.
Alcohol is an essential element of the music festival. Although lei tea contains alcohol, it is primarily a
traditional Hakka health tea that helps resolve phlegm and dissolve nodules, contributing to overall wellness. Additionally, for every 5 lei tea paper cups you collect, you can exchange them for an extra free cup of lei tea.
